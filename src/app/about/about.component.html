Get to know me

I am Lance Hardwood, the owner & principal photographer of Photographer Lance. I love being able to share the emotions and capture the vibe of all sorts of different events.

Based out of Vienna, Austria, I´ve been shooting sport events, travel experiences, portrait, architecture and animals for over ten years!
